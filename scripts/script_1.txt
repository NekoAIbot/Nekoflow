"Stock Markets Rattled by Latest Escalation in U.S.-China Trade War
The New York Times

The stock market had been on a tear on Tuesday, rising more than 3 percent and trading at nearly a 10-year high. The Dow Jones industrial average, which has been on a tear for almost a year, rose more than 6 percent, while the S&P 500 was up nearly 5 percent.

But a trade war between the United States and China has prompted investors to sell stocks, causing the S&P 500 to fall 2.6 percent, and the Dow to fall 2.2 percent, the most since August, according to the S&P Dow Jones Indices.
